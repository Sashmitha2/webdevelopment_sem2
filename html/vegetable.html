Vegetable

Vegetables are being bought from three main places for our supermarket.They are,

Local Farmer's Market
Local farmer's market is where freshly harvested vegetables are offered. Customers can engage directly with the sellers.We obtain vegetables from them at a very low price with good quality.
Regional Cooperative
This is where farmers adhere to organic environmetally friendly practices. Here vegtables are being grown with care resulting in high quality, nutrient-rich produce. The cooperative operates a farm store where customers can browse through bins brimming with carrots.
Wholesale Market
It is a warehouse where loading docks of vegetables take place. Trucks loaded with vegetables from farms near and far away are bought to our supermarket.It plays a crucial role in supplying our supermarket with abundance of fresh vegetables to meet the demands of customers.
